About Wings of Change

We are a community-based organization dedicated to empowering individuals and building stronger communities through education, support, and sustainable development.

Our Mission

Wings of Change exists to empower individuals and communities in Busia, Nambale Constituency through education, resources, and support programs that address immediate needs while building long-term resilience and self-sufficiency.

We believe that by working together, we can create lasting positive change that improves quality of life for all community members.

Our Vision

We envision thriving communities where all individuals have access to the resources, opportunities, and support they need to reach their full potential.

Through collaborative efforts and sustainable solutions, we work toward a future where economic, educational, and health disparities are eliminated.

Our History

From humble beginnings to a thriving community organization, our journey has been guided by a commitment to service and positive change.

2018

Our Beginning

Founded by a group of dedicated community members in Nambale, Wings of Change began as a small food pantry serving families in need. Our founders recognized that hunger was just one of many challenges facing our community.

Mid 2018

Expanding Our Reach

We launched our first educational support program, providing school supplies and tutoring to children from low-income families by offering them busaries. This marked the beginning of our holistic approach to community development.

Late of 2018

Community Center

We opened our first dedicated community center, providing a space for programs, workshops, and community gatherings. This milestone significantly expanded our capacity to serve the community.

We had established educational programs, job training initiatives, and youth mentorship opportunities. Our holistic approach to community development attracted support from local businesses, foundations, and government agencies.

2020

Pandemic Response

When COVID-19 hit, we quickly adapted our programs to meet emerging needs, providing emergency food assistance, health information, and support for remote learning.

Today

Continuing Our Mission

Today, Wings of Change serves thousands of individuals annually through our various programs. We continue to evolve and grow, guided by the needs of our community and our commitment to creating lasting positive change.
