Making a Difference in Nambale

At Wings of Change, we focus on sustainable initiatives that address the most pressing needs in our community. Through your support, we're able to create lasting positive change.

Education for All

Education is the foundation for a better future. In Nambale, many children face barriers to quality education due to poverty, lack of resources, and inadequate infrastructure. Our Education for All initiative aims to break down these barriers and provide every child with the opportunity to learn and grow.

  • Renovated classrooms in 5 local schools
  • Established a community library with over 1,000 books
  • Conducted teacher training programs to improve education quality

Community Food Bank

Food insecurity affects many families in Nambale, particularly during drought seasons and economic hardships. Our Community Food Bank initiative ensures that vulnerable families have access to nutritious meals and that no one in our community goes hungry.

Youth Development Program

Young people in Nambale face numerous challenges, including limited access to skills training, high unemployment rates, and few recreational opportunities. Our Youth Development Program empowers young people with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need to build bright futures for themselves and their communities.
